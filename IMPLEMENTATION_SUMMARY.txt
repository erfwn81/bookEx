Comment Model:
--------------
Fields:
- book (ForeignKey to Book)
- user (ForeignKey to User)
- text (TextField, max 500 chars)
- created_at (DateTimeField, auto_now_add)

Ordering: Most recent first
Related name: book.comments


Rating Model:
-------------
Fields:
- book (ForeignKey to Book)
- user (ForeignKey to User)
- stars (IntegerField, validators: 1-5)
- created_at (DateTimeField, auto_now_add)

Constraints: Unique together (book, user)
Ordering: Most recent first


Favorite Model:
---------------
Fields:
- user (ForeignKey to User, related_name='favorites')
- book (ForeignKey to Book)
- added_at (DateTimeField, auto_now_add)

Constraints: Unique together (user, book)
Ordering: Most recent first
